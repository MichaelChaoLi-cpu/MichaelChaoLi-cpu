## Current Focus

I build large-scale research data systems and AI models that connect global news, remote sensing, climate projections, ESG text, and well-being microdata.

| Research direction | What I am building |
| --- | --- |
| Climate and geoscience AI | Scenario-aware temperature prediction, climate impact modeling, gridded environmental datasets |
| Human well-being | Cross-survey harmonization, socio-environmental analysis, global microdata infrastructure |
| ESG and finance | News-driven ESG signals, corporate social-risk scoring, event-level market response modeling |
| Research software | Reproducible tools for spatial statistics, data harmonization, and AI-assisted empirical research |

## Research Infrastructure

| Stack | Scale and use |
| --- | --- |
| Global News Corpus | Approximately 200 million multilingual news articles with automated daily ingestion |
| Well-being Micro-Database | Gallup World Poll, Global Flourishing Study, DHS, and related survey alignment |
| Geospatial Data Stack | MODIS, CMIP6, population, land cover, air pollution, and greenness grids |
| AI Agent Workflows | Literature screening, variable alignment, robustness checking, and research automation |
| Compute | PostgreSQL data server, Google Cloud Platform, and shared GPU resources for large-model experiments |
